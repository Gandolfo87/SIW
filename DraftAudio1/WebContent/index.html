About DraftAudio

DraftAudio is a new generation platform to compose and share your music in a simple and effective way! In agreement with SafeCreative to protect the rights of the composeres, you can freely share your music without any problems or complications .

Take a look to our channel on Youtube for tutorial, demo and much more!

DraftAudio includes a fully functioning keyboard, which supports the latest MIDI protocol, connect your MIDI devices and free your inspiration!
